## [2020-06-30] (reflection, importance 8)
That cyclone took away months of work from the fields—I remember the fear in my stomach about how we would eat. The relief money was supposed to help us, but I heard stories of panchayat people taking their share before it reached families like mine. Still, I did not blame Didi for what those corrupt men did; she fights for us when she can, and it is the system that is rotten, not her.

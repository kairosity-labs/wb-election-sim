## [2020-06-30] (reflection, importance 8)
When the lockdown came, the cyclone came, and the border trade died all at once, I learned that neither Didi's hand nor Modi's promises reach a man like me when everything falls apart at the same time. The relief money meant for us never arrived — the panchayat people ate it — and I realized that even the schemes I voted for cannot protect me if the people who distribute them steal. I am still more afraid of NRC than BJP, but that period showed me I am alone either way.

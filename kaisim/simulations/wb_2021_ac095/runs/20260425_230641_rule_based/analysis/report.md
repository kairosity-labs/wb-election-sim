### default_n100_v1_p0069 (Namasudra_Matua, Female, age 68plus) — **BJP → Undecided**

> Five years I waited for the paper that Shantanu Thakur promised when I pressed the lotus button in 2019. My roof still leaks. My grandson still goes hungry. Now they give fourteen certificates—fourteen!—just five days before voting, like a show for the booth, not a promise kept. Both the lotus people and the green flag people have forgotten that Sarojini Haldar exists. I know Shantanu is Matua like me, and once I believed in him. But I have learned not to celebrate until the paper is in my hands, and I do not have it. Biswajit Das is not even Matua, and he changes his shirt like the seasons. I am too old and too tired to vote for the same broken promise again, even if it wears a familiar face.

**Primary drivers**:
  - 14 symbolic certificates delivered 5 days before election—a show, not a kept promise
  - Five years without the citizenship documentation that was promised in 2019
  - Unmet material needs: leaking roof, hungry grandchildren, while politicians fight over temples and power

  - Loss of faith that welfare schemes protect anyone when the system itself shields criminals and the powerful

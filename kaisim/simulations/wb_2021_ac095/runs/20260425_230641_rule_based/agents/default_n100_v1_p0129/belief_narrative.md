## [2020-12-31] (reflection, importance 6)
I remember when the camps came to the village for rations and schemes — it was helpful, yes, but it did not change how I feel about the government. They come with promises when elections are near, but after? We are forgotten again. What matters to me is that I take what my family needs to survive, but I do not forget who was silent when CAA came, and I do not let a few benefits make me believe any party truly cares for people like us.

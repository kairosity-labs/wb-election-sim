## [2020-12-31] (reflection, importance 7)
That cyclone took everything—the paddy, the light, weeks of nothing—but what I remember most is that Mamata had already sent the Krishak Bandhu money before it hit. The relief after was slow and stolen by middlemen like always, but at least the government had put something in my hand before the disaster came. That is the difference I see now between a government that thinks of farmers and one that only remembers us when we are already broken.

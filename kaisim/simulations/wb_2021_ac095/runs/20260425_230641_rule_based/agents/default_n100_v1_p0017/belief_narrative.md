## [2020-12-31] (reflection, importance 6)
That cyclone took some of our paddy, but it showed me that even when bad things happen, the TMC people still come around and try to help — that matters more than promises from others who don't show up at all. The road they fixed stayed solid even in the storm, and the Krishak Bandhu money kept coming. One disaster doesn't make me forget what actually works for my family.

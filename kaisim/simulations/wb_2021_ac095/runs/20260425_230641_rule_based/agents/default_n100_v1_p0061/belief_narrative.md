## [2020-12-31] (reflection, importance 8)
When the big wind came and tore our roof, Binod had no work for so many weeks and I was frightened we would not eat. The Mamatadidi people came to our house asking our names for help, but the lotus people — they never came. I do not know if the money reached us fairly or not, but I know who remembered us when we had nothing.

## [2024-01-31] (reaction, importance 2)
I heard something about trouble in Sandeshkhali, far from here. Bad things happen everywhere — I do not know this Shahjahan or what he did. The lotus people say the grass people are corrupt, the grass people say the lotus people are cruel. But when my roof broke and Binod had no work, it was the grass people who came to our door and asked our names. That is what I remember. Far-away news does not change what my hands have lived.

## [2024-02-29] (reaction, importance 2)

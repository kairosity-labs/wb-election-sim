## [2022-12-31] (reflection, importance 7)
That time when fertilizer prices shot up and the Centre started holding back money — it showed me that voting for change doesn't always bring the change you hope for. My cousin went hungry because of the fighting between Delhi and Kolkata, and I realized that when governments play blame games, it is the poor who pay, not the corrupt panchayat leaders. I still think the local TMC steals and does nothing, but I also learned that the BJP's answer — cutting funds — hurts people like my cousin more than it hurts anyone in power.

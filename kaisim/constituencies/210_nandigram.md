# AC 210 — Nandigram (GEN)

**District:** Purba Medinipur · **Region:** Purba Medinipur Coast · **LS:** Tamluk (PC 30) · **2026 Phase:** Phase 1 (23 April 2026, already polled) · **Total electors:** ~275,000 (2026 roll; 2021 was 257,992)

> Pilot #2 — tests the **2021 Mamata-vs-Suvendu symbolic seat + Mahishya-Muslim coalition + 2007 land-movement legacy + SIR Muslim-deletion controversy** axis.

## 1. Demographic snapshot

- Composition: **Nandigram-I CDB (10 GPs)** + **Nandigram-II CDB (7 GPs)** = 17 GPs (A, Delimitation 2008)
  - Nandigram-I GPs: Bhekutia, Daudpur, Gokulnagar, Haripur, Kalicharanpur, Kendemarijalpai, Mahammadpur, Nandigram, Samsabad, Sonachura
  - Nandigram-II GPs: Amdabad-I, Amdabad-II, Birulia, Boyal-I, Boyal-II, Khodambari-I, Khodambari-II
- Subdivision: Haldia
- **~97% rural** (Nandigram-I 2.79% urban + Nandigram-II 4.28% urban)
- **SIR deletions:** **2,826 names deleted** in final SIR, of which **~2,700 (95.5%) Muslim voters** + ~10,500 under adjudication (D, [The Week](https://www.theweek.in/news/india/2026/04/07/in-suvendu-adhikaris-nandigram-955percent-of-deleted-voters-are-muslims.html))

## 3. Caste composition

- **SC total: 16.46%** (AC weighted from Nandigram-I 18.58% / Nandigram-II 12.89%, A Census 2011)
- **ST: negligible 0.10%** (139 + 196 persons)
- **SC sub-composition (district E):** Bagdi ~30–35% of SC, Jalia Kaibartta ~15–20% of SC, Bhuinmali, Hari, Mal; **Namasudra minor** (Matua belt is Nadia/N24P)
- **Mahishya OBC (headline):** ~**45–55%** of population (~60–70% of Hindu population) (D/E) — **Adhikari family is Mahishya**; no census enumeration (WB hasn't extended OBC status to mainstream Mahishya, [Business Standard 2021](https://www.business-standard.com/article/current-affairs/nadda-promises-obc-status-for-left-out-hindu-backward-castes-in-bengal-121031601353_1.html))
- **Other OBC** (Muslim OBC, Teli, Tili, Tanti): ~5–8% (E)
- **UC bhadralok** (Brahmin/Kayastha/Baidya): ~8–12% (E, low — Nandigram is Mahishya-majority rural, not bhadralok)

## 4. Language

Bengali ≥98% (E, from district 98.31%). No notable Hindi/Urdu/Santhali pockets. Language not a cleavage.

## 5. Economic profile

- **Occupation mix (AC weighted 2011, A):**
  - Cultivators ~16%
  - Agricultural labourers **~46%** (Nandigram-II alone 50.33% — extreme dependence)
  - HH industry ~3.7%
  - Other workers ~34%
- **Agri household >60%** (E)
- **Fishing:** coastal AC on Haldi River estuary; Sonachura, Birulia GPs notable
- **BPL:** AC ~28–32% modelled (E, Census 2001 CDB baseline ~32–35% for erstwhile Midnapore rural)
- **Out-migration:** documented source region ([IJIRMF study](https://www.ijirmf.com/patterns-of-out-migration-from-rural-areas-a-case-study-of-purba-medinipur-district/)) — workers to Tamil Nadu textile (Tirupur/Chennai), Kerala construction, Maharashtra. AC estimate ~8–12% of working-age males (D/E)

## 9. Land-movement legacy (2007)

**14 March 2007:** police firing during resistance to Salim-group chemical-hub land acquisition; **14 killed, ~100 injured** (A). 18,123 acres notified across 29 mouzas (Nandigram-I + Khejuri-II) would have displaced ~95,000. Led by **Bhumi Uchhed Pratirodh Committee (BUPC)**.

**2026 mapping (C/D, [The Federal](https://thefederal.com/elections-2026/nandigram-west-bengal-elections-2026-hindutva-ground-zero-bjp-trinamool-suvendu-adhikari-240182)):**
- Cross-communal BUPC unity has **eroded**. Suvendu reframes the constituency as **"Hindutva ground zero"**; religious identity now dominates over land/livelihood discourse
- Land-movement martyrs' families split BJP/TMC
- Muslim 26% bloc consolidated behind TMC since 2019; Hindu Mahishya-Bagdi ~55–60% partially shifted to BJP
- Net: the 1,956-vote 2021 margin is structural, not a fluke — ~2–3% cross-communal swing from the 2007–08 coalition

**Headline.** Bharatpur is **~58% Muslim** — notably *below* Murshidabad district's 66.27% (prompt baseline). Federal reporting quotes "Bharatpur: 58% Muslim voters" consistent with block data ([The Federal](https://thefederal.com/elections-2026/babri-mosque-politics-murshidabad-belt-2026-bengal-elections-237784)). The Hindu ~42% includes a thin **Namasudra-refugee undercurrent** along the Bhagirathi (share lower than Nadia/N24P but non-trivial).

**Critical structural note.** Kabir himself is **not on the Bharatpur ballot** — he contests Rejinagar (AC 70). AJUP's AC-69 effect is therefore **indirect**: routed through (i) the two Muslim-named independents (Anowar Hossain Molla, Abu Kowsar Malek Ismile) absorbing Kabir-sympathetic votes, (ii) reduced TMC booth-worker discipline where Kabir loyalists sit out, (iii) INC pull-off (Azheruddin Md). Model AJUP as **~3–8 pp Muslim-vote drag on TMC**, routed to INC + independents — *not* as a direct AJUP vote column. Per The Federal: *"Kabir still retains influence in Bharatpur despite no longer being its candidate"*.

### Causal forces & SCM linkage

1. **Muslim fragmentation** (primary axis, **Very High**) — TMC 2021 Muslim share ≈75%; 2026 has 4–5 Muslim-aligned options (TMC/INC/AJUP-aligned inds/CPI-ML/ISF-thin). 10–15 pp fragmentation survivable at 58% base; Hindu 42% + BJP consolidation narrows margin.
2. **SIR deletion drag** (**Very High**) — ~23–30k AC deletions; Muslim-skewed; hits out-migrant males + documentation-gap Muslim women (TMC-leaning). Direct floor-depress.
3. **Hindu 42% + BJP polarization** (High) — Not win-alone, but Kabir's Babri foundation gifts BJP opp-culture ammo; BJP floor ~25%+.
4. **Welfare stack floor** (Very High) — 114,606 female electors + near-universal LB/Khadya/Swasthya + Imam-Muezzin + Jamiat = TMC arithmetic Kabir cannot match.
5. **INC residual** (Moderate-High) — Adhir's Baharampur capillaries; Azheruddin Md can pull 10–15% off TMC — drag distinct from AJUP.
6. **Duare Sarkar × SIR** (High) — camps function as TMC's citizenship-documentation mitigation.
7. **Out-migrant workers** (High) — SIR "absent" deletions over-represented here.
8. Matua/Namasudra low (~5–7%); CAA beneficiaries low (Muslim-majority, CAA cuts anti-BJP); UC bhadralok low; SSC-scam youth low; border narrative district-level not AC.

### Axis-specific simulation priors

Treat AC 69 as a **three-way Muslim-vote-fragmentation test case**:
- **TMC floor** (Muslim + LB-women + Imam-Jamiat): ~44–48%
- **BJP ceiling** (Hindu consolidation): ~28–33%
- **INC** (Azheruddin Md, Gani-Khan residual): ~10–14%
- **AJUP-indirect drag on TMC** (via Muslim inds + depressed turnout): 3–8 pp off TMC
- **Left-morcha** (CPI-ML Jinnatun Nahar): 2–4%
- **Others / independents**: 2–4%

**Central scenario:** TMC hold with margin compression to **10–25k** (vs 43k in 2021). **Tail risk:** compound SIR-exclusion + AJUP-drag + BJP-consolidation could compress margin into single-digit thousands — but outright flip remains low-probability given 58% Muslim base. **Alt tail:** Kabir loses Rejinagar decisively and AJUP collapses statewide, in which case AC 69 margin stays near 40k.

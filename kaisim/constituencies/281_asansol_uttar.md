# AC 281 — Asansol Uttar (GEN)

**District:** Paschim Bardhaman · **Region:** Burdwan Industrial · **LS:** Asansol (PC 42) · **2026 Phase:** TBD · **Total electors:** per CEO WB SIR 2026 row

> Pilot #9 — tests the **industrial Hindi-speaker + Bengali bhadralok + urban BJP-competitive** axis. Paschim Bardhaman is WB's most urbanised district (81.61% urban, tier A).

## 1. Demographic snapshot

- Composition: northern wards of **Asansol Municipal Corporation** (Railpar, Hutton Road, Rahalane, Apcar Garden, Murgasol, Ushagram, Dhadka, Asansol Bazar), plus fringes of Asansol-Durgapur CDB (A, Delimitation 2008)
- **~95%+ urban** — wholly municipal AC, no rural tail (E)
- LS segments: AC 281 grouped with Pandabeswar, Raniganj, Jamuria, Asansol Dakshin, Kulti, Barabani in 42-Asansol LS

**Bilingual media ecosystem:** Dainik Jagran, Hindustan (Hindi) + Bartaman, Anandabazar Patrika (Bengali) — unique in WB outside Kolkata. Amplifies BJP-favoured national narratives.

## 4. Economic profile

Four legacy industrial pillars (all A, Wikipedia + sector sources):

1. **Coal** — Eastern Coalfields Ltd (ECL), Sanctoria HQ adjacent
2. **Steel** — IISCO Steel Plant (Burnpur, SAIL-ISP), 2.5 MTPA expansion ~2015
3. **Railways** — Asansol Division of Eastern Railway; Asansol Junction loco/crew hub
4. **Trade & services** — commercial capital of western WB (hinterland: Dhanbad-Purulia-Bankura)

**Decline narrative:** stagnant-to-mild-decline since 1990s; youth out-migration to Bengaluru/Pune/Gulf. Drives anti-incumbency.

## 5. Education

- AC literacy ~84–87% (E, above district 78.75% — male 85.44 / female 65.55, tier A)
- Kazi Nazrul University (Asansol), BB College, Asansol Engineering College, St. Vincent's, Loreto Convent, DAV chain
- **English-medium penetration** among highest in non-Kolkata WB

## 6. Welfare footprint

Urban seat → welfare is **second-order** vs identity/language/industrial-decline narratives. Lakshmir Bhandar reaches working-class Hindi-speaker + Muslim households (TMC floor). PMAY-Urban, Ujjwala, Ayushman Bharat generate BJP credit in Hindi-speaker wards. SAIL/ECL/Railways pension economy → anti-incumbent lean.

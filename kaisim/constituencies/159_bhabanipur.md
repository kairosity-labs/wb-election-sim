# AC 159 — Bhabanipur (GEN)

**District:** Kolkata · **Region:** Kolkata Metro (Kolkata South) · **2026 Phase:** Phase 2 (29 April 2026) · **Lok Sabha:** 23 Kolkata Dakshin · **Total electors (post-SIR, Feb 2026):** ~1,59,201 (final roll) / 1,61,508 (draft)

> Urban-bhadralok + Hindi-speaker + minority-mix axis. The seat of CM Mamata Banerjee — won at the 2021 byelection after her Nandigram loss, now her own nomination for 2026. A "mini-India" AC: three roughly-equal blocs (Bengali Hindu, non-Bengali Hindu, Muslim) inside eight KMC wards. Every cell carries a confidence tier (A–E per `/data/methodology.md` §1). Tier-D/E values are flagged; do not treat them as measurements.

Bengali is a **plurality, not majority** in Bhabanipur — a defining contrast with virtually every other non-KMC seat. This interacts directly with TMC's "Bengali asmita" vs BJP's "double-engine for national-language trader class" axis.

### 2024 LS segment — critical detail

Mala Roy retained Kolkata Dakshin by ~1.87 lakh votes overall but **trailed the BJP inside the Bhabanipur AC segment and inside adjacent Rashbehari (AC 160)**. BJP has consistently secured strong vote shares in 6 of Bhabanipur's 8 KMC wards since 2014 LS, losing only wards 77 and 82/63 (Muslim concentration). The TMC's pan-state welfare stack and Mamata's personal candidature are what shifted the 2021 bypoll — not a structural TMC advantage in the AC segment.

### Causal forces running through AC 159

1. **SIR compression of the electorate** — 47,094 names struck off (22.8% of the 2021 roll). TMC claims disproportionate hit in wards 70, 72, 77. Ward 70 and 72 are Marwari/Gujarati-dominant (ostensibly BJP-favorable), but ward 77 is Muslim-majority (TMC-favorable) — **the net partisan effect is genuinely ambiguous**, which is why both parties are door-to-door auditing. The adjudication-pending 14,154 names will swing the final picture.
2. **Bengali vs non-Bengali frame** — Mamata's "Bengali asmita" has under-penetrated the Marwari/Gujarati ~20% block since 2014; Suvendu's candidacy is calibrated to maximize non-Bengali consolidation behind BJP. TMC's counter is Sovandeb Chattopadhyay's local machine plus welfare-stack reach into Bengali Hindu + Muslim households.
3. **Muslim consolidation (~22–24%)** — ward 77 is the single heaviest TMC-floor ward; any Muslim turnout drop or ISF splinter would immediately cost TMC the seat. 2021 bypoll Muslim consolidation ≈95% TMC.
4. **UC Bengali bhadralok floating vote** — historically INC → CPI(M) → TMC; since 2019 a measurable share has drifted BJP (SSC-scam, corruption narrative). This ~30% bloc is the swing.
5. **Personal-candidate incumbency effect** — Mamata's name on the ballot added ~14 percentage points over the Sovandeb 2021 AE baseline at the 2021 bypoll (47.67% → 71.90%). The 2026 assumption is a similar personal uplift, but the 2024 LS segment loss suggests the uplift is candidate-specific, not party-structural.

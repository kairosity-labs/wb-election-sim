## 2. Caste composition

AC-level tier-E estimates; WB caste classification treats most Nepali-origin hill communities as GEN or OBC rather than SC/ST, so district rollup (tier E) under-captures structure.

| Group | AC 23 estimate (%) | Tier | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| SC total | 6–8 | E | Well below state 23%; mainly Kami, Damai, Sarki (Nepali Dalit) |
| **ST total** | 8–12 | E | Bhutia, Lepcha, Sherpa, **Tamang (ST since 2003)**, Limbu (ST) |
| UC/General (Bahun, Chhetri, Thakuri) | ~25–30 | E | Politically dominant in GJM/GNLF/BGPM leadership |
| OBC (Rai, Gurung, Mangar, Newar, Jogi, Bhujel, Sunuwar) | ~30–35 | E | WB OBC-A/B notified |

**Key:** ethnic-linguistic Gorkha identity overrides caste arithmetic in voting.

## 4. Economic profile

- **Tea estates (20+ in/around AC):** Happy Valley, Risheehat, Lingia, Badamtam, Tukvar, Rungmook-Cedars, Pandam, Puttabong, Phoobsering (A)
- **Tea wage:** ₹250/day (2025 notification); persistent grievance, TMC 2026 pitch ₹300/day (B/D, WB Labour Dept + [ThePrint](https://theprint.in/politics/how-tea-gardens-in-north-bengal-key-to-poll-fortunes-of-bjp-tmc-have-kept-both-guessing/641016/))
- **Tourism:** Darjeeling town + Tiger Hill + Batasia + DHR (UNESCO World Heritage); ~1.5–2M visitors/year pre-COVID (C/D)
- **Horticulture:** orange, cardamom, ginger, floriculture (D)
- **Remittances:** outflow to Gulf, Singapore, Delhi, Bengaluru hospitality (D/E)

## 6. Welfare scheme footprint (dual layer)

**State WB:** Lakshmir Bhandar (measurable anti-BJP drag among hill women per journalistic reports); Kanyashree, Rupashree, Sabuj Sathi, Swasthya Sathi. Hill uptake generally lower than plains due to administrative friction.

**GTA layer:** 14 Development Boards (Tamang, Sherpa, Limbu, Rai, Mangar, Gurung, Bhujel, Newar, Yolmo, Thami, Khas-Brahmin etc.) created 2014–17 by state govt to bypass GTA — politically charged patronage instrument.

**2026 three-way:**
- **BJP-GNLF:** likely re-field Neeraj Zimba or rotate GNLF/BJP face
- **TMC-BGPM alliance:** BGPM candidate on BGPM symbol (TMC conceded 3 hill seats)
- **Hamro Party (Ajoy Edwards):** swept Darjeeling Municipality 2022 (18/32 wards); urban floor ~25–35% (A/D)

**GTA 2022:** BGPM 27/45; Hamro 8; TMC 5. BJP+GNLF boycotted.

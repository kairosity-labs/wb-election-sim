## 5. Economic profile

- **BPL (2007 Paschim Medinipur DHDR):** Binpur-II **68.59%** / Jamboni **67.11%** → AC ~68% (E). Among WB's poorest belts.
- **Land:** 95% of Binpur-II cultivated land lateritic (infertile); Jamboni 85% lateritic (A, Wikipedia CDB)
- **Forest cover ~23% of CDB area** — sal, mahua, kendu leaf, lac livelihood
- **Agri workers dominant:** Jamboni — patta holders 36.6%, bargadars 4.69%, smallholders 2.34%
- **MGNREGA funding frozen from Dec 2021** → out-migration surge to Maharashtra, TN, AP brick-kilns, Punjab paddy harvest (C, The Federal)

## 7. Welfare footprint

Heavy ST/SC scheme recipient base:
- **Jai Johar ST pension** (₹1,000/mo, 60+ ST) — ~35% of AC is ST → large beneficiary pool
- **Tapasili Bandhu SC pension** (₹1,000/mo, 60+ SC) — ~17% of AC SC
- **Lakshmir Bhandar** ₹1,200/mo (SC/ST) — near-universal uptake, huge TMC political lever in Jangalmahal
- **Banglar Bari, Krishak Bandhu, Sabuj Sathi, Kanyashree, Sikshashree** — widely rolled out

Jhargram is among WB's highest Lakshmir Bhandar saturation districts given ST female demographic weight.
